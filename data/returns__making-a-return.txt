## Start a return

Return items within 365 days of purchase with free return shipping.

To start a return:
- Open the Chewy app.
- Go to Order history in your account and select the order with the item you want to return.
- On the item you want to return, select Start a return.

If you aren't able to use the Chewy app, chat with us or call us at 1-800-672-4399 to start a return.

Most items are returnable. See our return policy for more details.

Prescription items: To return prescription items, please contact us for more details. Chat live or call us at 1-877-977-3879.

================================================================

## Return status

- Open the Chewy App.
- Go to Orders in your account and select the order with the item you returned.
- There will be details about your return and any next steps.

We'll send you emails with your return label, to notify you when your refund is processed to your Chewy gift card balance, or when a replacement item is on its way to you.

### Refund timing
- Refunds to your Chewy gift card balance or Chewy+ rewards take 15 minutes to process. Refunds to your Chewy gift card balance are subject to the Chewy Gift Cards Terms & Conditions.
- Refunds to other payment methods can take up to 5 business days.
- For PayPal refunds, it can take up to 30 business days.

If you have questions about the status of your return, chat live or call us at 1-800-672-4399.
