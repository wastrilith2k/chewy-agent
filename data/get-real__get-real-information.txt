## Storing and feeding Get Real

### Storing and thawing Get Real
Keep it frozen to keep it fresh. Packs are pre-portioned based on dogs' nutritional needs for easy serving and storage.

Thaw Get Real in the fridge for 12-24 hours for the mixer (5 oz) and 12 oz packs, or 35-48 hours for the 24 oz packs. If you need thawed food faster, put the sealed pack in a cold water bath to thaw. Use it up within 3-5 days. Throw away food that smells bad or has been out at room temp for more than 2 hours.

### Switching your dog to Get Real
Start by gradually adding a small amount of Get Real to your dog's current food. Increase the amount of Get Real and decrease the current food over the next 7-10 days. If you're using Get Real to top your dog's current food, start with a small amount and increase over time. Be sure to mix it in! For sensitive dogs, we recommend transitioning over 10-14 days.

If yours doesn't love Get Real after the transition, we can send you different recipes to try, or you can chat with us or call us at 1-800-672-4399 for a refund.

## Change or cancel Get Real

You can change your plan anytime from your plan management page. Try new flavors or change the amount or frequency of your orders.

You can change or cancel a future order up until the time we start packing your order. We'll send you a reminder before your next scheduled order with a link to change or cancel.

Ways you can change your plan:
- Skip an order: If you're not ready for your next delivery, select Skip order.
- Change your next order date: Select a new date and confirm.
- Update delivery frequency: Choose a new frequency from the dropdown.
- Cancel: To cancel your Get Real subscription, scroll to the bottom and select Cancel.
