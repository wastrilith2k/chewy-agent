## Chewy+ rewards

### Earning rewards

You'll earn rewards automatically every time you place an eligible order. Chewy+ members can view available rewards on the Chewy homepage and in the Chewy+ section in account.

To earn rewards:
- Sign in to your Chewy account.
- Place an order with eligible items. (Excludes gift cards and pet insurance plans.)
- After your order ships, 5% of your subtotal (before taxes and fees) will be added to your rewards balance.

Chewy+ members can view available rewards on the Chewy homepage and in the Chewy+ section in account.

### Redeeming rewards

To redeem rewards:
- Add items to your cart.
- At checkout, look for the Apply rewards option.
- Select Apply to use your available rewards.

Rewards are valid for 12 months from the date earned and expire at the end of that month. There is a $3,000 annual spending limit for earning rewards, so any purchases beyond that won't accumulate additional rewards until your membership renews.

## Cancel Chewy+

If Chewy+ isn't right for you, it's easy to end your membership.

To end your membership:
- Sign in to your account.
- In your account, go to Chewy+ membership.
- Select Manage membership.
- Choose End membership, then follow the prompts.

Your benefits will continue through the end of your billing period. You won't be charged again unless you rejoin.
