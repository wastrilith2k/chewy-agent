## Donate to a rescue

Use our Donate to a rescue search to find a specific organization or organizations near you and donate items through their wish list.

================================================================

## Find pets to adopt

Search thousands of available pets from shelters and rescues in Chewy's network. Refine your search to find the perfect match and complete the adoption process at your local shelter or rescue.

================================================================

## Donate a Chewy eGift card

### Donating an eGift card
- Use our Donate to a rescue search to find a specific organization near you.
- Select the Chewy eGift card from their wish list.
- Select the amount and fill in any personalization details, then add it to your cart and check out.

### Viewing past donations
View the eGift cards donated in your order history. All of your donation orders and personal orders will be listed here.

### Getting a refund for a donated eGift card
Please chat with us or call us 24/7 at 1-800-672-4399 and we'll be happy to help.

================================================================

## Information shared in donations

For privacy and security purposes, we do not share your information with the group you're donating to.

If you wish to make them aware of your donation, we suggest reaching out to the organization directly, using the contact information located on their page.

================================================================

## Tax-deductible donations

If you'd like to claim the donation on your taxes, please save a copy of your order invoice and speak to your tax advisor.

================================================================

## Donate in the Chewy cart

The organizations featured are randomly selected. At this time, a specific rescue cannot be chosen. If you'd like to support a particular rescue, use our Donate to a rescue search.
