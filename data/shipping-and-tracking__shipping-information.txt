## Shipping cost and timing

Most orders ship fast and free—here’s what to know.

### Free shipping for orders over $49
Orders with a subtotal of $49 or more (before tax and discounts) ship free.
For smaller orders, flat-rate shipping is $4.95.

### Delivery timelines
Most customers receive their order in 1 to 3 days. You’ll get a shipping confirmation with tracking details within 24 hours of placing your order. If your order includes items that ship directly from vendors, there will be a date range for expected arrival and your tracking details will be sent when your item ships.

For orders with prescription items, customers can expect delivery within 3 to 5 days. This includes the time needed to receive vet approval, which typically takes 1 day.

## Shipping carriers

We ship with FedEx, OnTrac, and USPS.

Delivery windows typically range from 8 a.m. to 9 p.m., depending on the carrier.

## Frozen or refrigerated shipping

### Our packaging
We ship refrigerated and frozen items separately from other items in your order to help them arrive in the best possible condition.

We use insulated packaging and coolant such as dry ice or gel packs to keep items refrigerated or frozen. Frozen items are packed with Climacell insulation, which is curbside recyclable.

### Keeping your order cold
Depending on when your order is placed, it may take a few days to leave our fulfillment center. This helps us make sure your frozen items spend the shortest possible time in transit. Our packaging is designed to keep things safely cold for up to 72 hours once packed.

### What to expect when your package arrives
- There should be no sign of leaks. You may see frost or condensation—that’s normal and means the packaging kept the item cold.
- Ice packs may partially melt or the dry ice may disappear.

## Primary shipping address

### Updating your primary shipping address
If you've already added multiple shipping addresses to your account, here's how to set one as your primary:
- Sign in to your Chewy account.
- In the Addresses section, find the address you'd like to set as your primary.
- Select the checkbox to make this your primary address and Save address.
- You’ll see a confirmation that your primary address has been updated.

### Updating your primary shipping address with a new address
To add a new address and make it your primary:
- Sign in to your Chewy account.
- Select the Addresses section.
- Select Add an address.
- Check the box to use this as your primary shipping address.
- Fill out your name, street address, city, state, ZIP code, and phone number.
- Check the box next to Make this my primary shipping address.
- Select Save address.

Your new address will now be set as the primary for future orders.

## International shipping

We now ship to parts of Canada through Chewy.com/ca. Currently, we deliver to Southern Ontario, including:
- Toronto
- Hamilton
- Niagara
- Ottawa
- Sudbury
- North Bay

We ship to postal codes that begin with the letters L, M, N, and K.

To place an order in Canada, head to Chewy.com/ca and start shopping. You'll find a wide selection of pet essentials and an experience tailored for Canadian pet parents.

## Shipping restrictions

We’re unable to ship Chewy orders to:
- PO boxes
- APO/FPO addresses
- Alaska, Hawaii, or locations outside the contiguous U.S (except some parts of Canada).
