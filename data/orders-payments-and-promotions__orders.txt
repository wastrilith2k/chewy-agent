## Place an order

You can place an order for your pet's favorite items in just a few steps. Here's what to know when you're ordering prescription items for your pet.
- Sign in or create your Chewy account.
- Search or browse for the item you want to purchase.
- Select the item to view its details. Choose the quantity or size, if needed. Select Add to cart.
- When you're ready, select the cart icon, then select Proceed to checkout.
- Confirm your shipping address.
- Choose your payment method.
- Review your order.
- Select Place order.

Once your order is placed, you'll see a confirmation screen with the details. You'll receive an order confirmation email and a tracking number within 24 hours.

## Place a guest order

You can shop, add items to your cart, and check out as a guest. If you're ordering prescription items or veterinary diets or if you want to start an Autoship, you'll need to create a Chewy account for that.

To check out and place an order without creating an account:
- Search or browse for the item you want to purchase.
- Select the item to view its details. Choose the quantity or size, if needed. Select Add to cart.
- When you're ready, select the cart icon, then select Proceed to checkout.
- In checkout, enter your name and email address and select Continue, or choose to use Google Pay, Apple Pay, or PayPal.
- Enter your shipping address then select Ship to this address.
- Choose your payment method and enter your payment details.
- Review your order and select Place order.

Once your order is placed, you'll see a confirmation screen with the details. You'll receive an order confirmation email and a tracking number within 24 hours.

## Back in stock notification

To sign up for notifications:
- On the item page, you'll see an Out of stock message.
- Enter your email address in the field provided.
- Select Notify me.

Once you've signed up, we'll send you an email as soon as the item is back in stock so you can order it.

## Cancel an order

You can cancel an order as long as it hasn't started processing for shipment. Once it begins processing, cancellation is no longer available.

To cancel an order:
- Sign in to your account.
- Go to Order history under Account.
- Find the order you want to cancel and select Order details.
- Select Cancel this order.
- Confirm your cancellation.

If the Cancel this order option isn't available, the order is already processing or has shipped. You may be able to return the items once you receive them.

## Order invoices

Whether you need an invoice for a donation receipt, filing a pet insurance claim, or any reason, invoices are found in your order history while on your desktop browser.
- Sign in to your Chewy account.
- In account, select Order history.
- Select Order details for the order you want an invoice for.
- Select the View invoice button. This will download a copy of your invoice to your device that you can view, save, or print.
