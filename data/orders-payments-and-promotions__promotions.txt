## Apply a Chewy promo code

- Add items to your cart and go to checkout.
- In checkout, find the Add promo code field.
- Enter your promotion code.
- Select Apply.

You'll see the discount reflected in your order total if the code is valid and your order is eligible.

Some codes have restrictions. Check the terms on Chewy or in the email or mailer where you received the code.

================================================================

## Discounted prices

You may see strikethrough prices like this while shopping for your pet: $14.99. The information icon next to the price will have details about the type of price it is.

Was price: This price is determined by the recent price history of an item sold by Chewy. The was price is a price that was offered for at least 30 of the past 90 days.

List price: This is the suggested retail price of an item from a manufacturer, supplier, or other retailer. We show a list price for products offered by other retailers at or above the list price in the recent past. It may not necessarily reflect the prevailing or regular retail price of the product.

## Manufacturer coupons

Our customer care team can process a manufacturer's coupon. Please place your order and mail your coupon to:

Attn: Chewy Coupons
7700 W Sunrise Blvd
Plantation, FL 33322

Be sure to include:
- The original coupon
- Your email address
- Your recent order number (from your order confirmation email or order details)

We cannot accept expired, copied, scanned, emailed, competitor, or rebate coupons.

================================================================

## Senior and military discounts

We don't currently offer senior or military discounts, but you can always check Today's deals for savings.
