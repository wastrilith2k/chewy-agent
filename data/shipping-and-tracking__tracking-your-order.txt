## Tracking order status

### In your account
Go to Order history in your account. Here's what you'll see:
- Preparing for shipment: This means we're currently processing and packing the items for your pet. You'll receive an email to let you know we received your order. When your order ships, we'll send another email with tracking information.
- Shipped: Your order has left our warehouse. Within 24 hours, you will also see a link to track your package.
- Delivered: Your order has arrived! Most orders are delivered 7 days a week before 10 p.m.

Note: Items usually ship the same day but can sometimes take up to 48 hours to ship from when you place your order. Most customers receive their order in 1 to 3 days.

If your order includes a prescription item, delivery may take longer due to the approval process. Learn more about the prescription approval process.

### Emails we'll send about your order
- Thanks for your Chewy order: We received your order.
- Your package is on the way: We shipped your package and sent your tracking number via email.
- Your package arrives today: Expect your delivery that day.
- Your package has arrived: Your package was delivered.

## Lost Chewy package

If your order hasn't arrived and tracking stops updating, the shipping carrier may consider it lost. Once we confirm with the carrier that a package is lost, we'll make it right with a replacement or a refund, depending on the item and what works best for you (subject to availability).

If the original package unexpectedly shows up later, you're welcome to keep it or donate it — except for prescription (Rx) items, which should be returned or discarded. If your order includes prescription medication and you're concerned about a delay or a potentially lost package, please contact us right away.

If your package is delayed but continues to make progress in the tracking history, it isn't considered lost yet. We'll need to wait a bit longer to ensure it's either delivered or truly lost. Delays like this often happen during weather events (even if the weather looks fine where you are).
