## Shelters and rescues network

### Our network
Chewy's shelters and rescues network was founded in 2012 to provide support for shelters, rescues, and other qualified animal-centric non-profit organizations across the United States. There is absolutely no cost and no commitment to join our network.

### Benefits of joining
By participating in the network, your organization is eligible to create a custom wish list on Chewy to share with supporters for donations, promote your adoptable pets to our customers considering adoption, and earn referral commissions for every new customer who places their first order on Chewy.

### Eligibility
All qualified animal welfare non-profit 501(c)(3) organizations are eligible. While Chewy is open to partnering with almost all animal welfare organizations, we do reserve the right to refuse any organization that does not meet our requirements.

### How to join
Learn more about our shelters and rescues network, including how to join, or contact us at rescue@chewy.com.

## Managing eGift Card donations

### Automatically add donated eGift cards to gift card balance
To have your eGift cards added to your Chewy shelter account automatically:
- Navigate to your Shelter account.
- Edit the eGift card from the Wish List tab.

You'll still receive an email notification that an eGift card has been donated. The funds will be automatically added to your gift card balance.

Once a Chewy eGift card has been added to your account, it cannot be transferred to another account.

### Checking your gift card balance
- Navigate to your Shelter account.
- Select the Gift Cards tab.

You'll see the current available funds, along with a breakdown of any eGift cards used and their subsequent orders.

To view all of the eGift cards donated, visit the Donations tab of your shelter account. You can filter donations further to show only eGift cards.

At this time, Chewy is unable to facilitate direct monetary donations from your supporters.

### Accidentally deleting an eGift card
Confirm the donation order number of the eGift card from the Donations tab of your shelter account. Once you have the donation order number, please chat live with us or call us at 1-800-672-4399 24/7 and we'll be happy to help.

## Discounts for non-profits

Although at this time we are unable to offer any general discounts or pricing, we do try to keep our prices competitive and have free shipping on all orders over $49.

Autoship is a great way to set up items on repeat deliveries, and there are certain brands that qualify for an additional 5% discount when added to an Autoship.

================================================================

## Tax exemption for non-profits

If your organization qualifies for sales tax exemption in the state it resides, please contact rescue@chewy.com to find out what documentation needs to be provided for us to update your organization's account.

## Rescues in the Chewy cart

Only approved partners in our Shelters and Rescues Network will be featured. The organizations featured are randomly selected. At this time, a specific rescue cannot be chosen.

If you're not sure if your organization is enrolled, use our Donate to a rescue search or email rescue@chewy.com.

## In-kind donations

In addition to the referral contributions from our shelters and rescues network, we've partnered with Greater Good Charities to provide partners with donations when needed. To register as a recipient of these donations, please apply through greatergood.org/goods.
