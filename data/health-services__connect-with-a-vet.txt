## Connect with a Vet overview

### How our online vet team can help
Connect with a Vet is Chewy's service providing telehealth for dogs and cats. Online vet visits with our team of vets and vet techs are a fast, easy way to get answers for your pet-related questions and concerns.

We cannot diagnose or treat medical conditions or prescribe medications for your pet.

Use our live chat to get free online vet help for questions like:
- "My dog ate chocolate [or socks, or sneakers—you know how it is]. Does he need emergency care?"
- "My cat has been scratching at her ears this week. How can I get her some relief?"
- "My dog is showing signs of separation anxiety. What can I do to calm her down when I leave the house?"

In addition to providing guidance, our online vets and vet techs can help you understand the urgency of your pet's situation and advise you about seeking in-person or emergency care. If your pet is experiencing a life-threatening situation, please go directly to the closest veterinary clinic.

### Cost and hours
Connect with a Vet is currently available to Chewy customers nationwide, except customers residing in Alaska, Hawaii, or Puerto Rico.

Live chat is free.

The price for a single video appointment (15-minutes) is $19.99.

Video calls are free with certain CarePlus pet insurance plans (not available for CarePlus 'Accident Only' or 'Wellness' plan customers).

Our expert telehealth team is here 365 days a year, including holidays.
- Live chat consults with an online vet or vet tech are available from 6 a.m. to 12 a.m. Eastern Time.
- Video consults are available from 8 a.m. to 11 p.m. Eastern Time.
