Title: Creating or updating Autoship

Topics on this page:
- Start a new Autoship
- Change an Autoship order
- Cancel an Autoship

================================================================

## Start a new Autoship

- Sign in to your account.
- Find the item you want and select Set up Autoship.
- In checkout, choose Autoship and select your preferred delivery frequency.

We'll send you an order confirmation email and an email when your order ships.

When your next order is coming up, we'll send you an email reminder. As a valued Autoship customer, we prioritize your orders with reserved inventory.

After you start an Autoship, you can manage your upcoming orders in your Chewy account.

================================================================

## Change an Autoship order

Update items, change your delivery date, or cancel your Autoship anytime.

To manage your Autoship:
- Go to Manage Autoship in your account.
- Choose the Autoship you want to update.

From here, you can:
- Change your next order date: Select a new date and confirm.
- Update delivery frequency: Choose a new frequency from the dropdown.
- Order now: Place an order for an item you need before your Autoship date.
- Skip an order: If you're not ready for your next Autoship, select Skip order.
- Add, skip, or remove items: Search for new items to add, skip items for an order, or remove items you no longer need.
- Change shipping address or payment: Select Edit next to the address or payment info.
- Cancel Autoship: Scroll to the bottom and select Cancel Autoship. You can always create a new one later.

================================================================

## Cancel an Autoship

You can cancel an Autoship anytime from your account. Once canceled, you won't be charged or receive future deliveries from that Autoship.

To cancel an Autoship:
- Sign in to your account.
- Go to Manage Autoship.
- Select the Autoship you want to cancel.
- Scroll to the bottom and select Cancel Autoship.
- Confirm your cancellation when prompted.

You'll receive an email confirming the cancellation.

Quick tip: If you're not ready for your next Autoship, you can skip a delivery instead. Just select Skip order from the Autoship details page.
