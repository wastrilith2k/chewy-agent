### Accreditations

Chewy Pharmacy has NABP (National Association of Boards of Pharmacy) and LegitScript accreditation.

Chewy Compounding Pharmacy has achieved PCAB Accreditation, so you can be assured that our pharmacy is committed to meeting national quality standards.

## Chewy Pharmacy sourcing

We source our prescription items and compounding ingredients from manufacturers registered with the FDA and distributors licensed by states.

Our priority is your pet's health, so we carry the same high-quality prescription items found in vet clinics.
