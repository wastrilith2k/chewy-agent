## Autoship overview

Autoship is an easy way to set up repeat deliveries from Chewy and save on the items you buy regularly.

### Easy to manage
Your orders ship automatically on a schedule you choose. You can update the date, items, address, or payment method any time in Manage Autoship in your account. We'll notify you before we prepare an Autoship order in case you want to make any changes.

### Ways to save
- 35% off your first Autoship (up to $20)
- 5% off future Autoship orders
- See your total Autoship savings in Manage Autoship

Some exclusions apply. Learn more and shop Autoship-eligible items.
