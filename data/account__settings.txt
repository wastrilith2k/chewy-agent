## Update your account

Update your account details anytime—like your name, email, phone number, or password—to keep everything current.

- Sign in to your Chewy account.
- Select Account.
- Under Settings & preferences, choose the section you want to edit.
- Make your updates.
- Select Save changes.

### Things to know
- If you update your email, you'll be signed out and asked to sign in again with the new address.
- Changing your password will also log you out of all devices for security.
- Changing or removing your mobile number will opt you out of any text messages you opted in to receive.

## Reset your Chewy password

To reset your password by email:
- Go to the Sign in page.
- Enter the email address for your Chewy account and select Continue.
- If you forgot your password or want to reset it, select Forgot password?
- Confirm your email address and select Reset password.
- Check your inbox for a reset password email and follow the link.
- Enter a new password and confirm it.
- Select Save to complete the reset.

Another option to reset your password is to sign in to your Chewy account with a one-time code and change your password in account settings.

### Tips for a strong password
- Use at least 8 characters, including a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.
- Avoid common words or easily guessed phrases—don't use "password," "123456," or your pet's name.
- Don't reuse passwords from other accounts.

## Notification preferences

- Sign in to your account.
- From your account, select Settings & preferences, then Notification preferences.
- Expand the text messages or email section to update your preferences. On the Chewy app, you'll also have a push notifications section.
- For text messages: If you have a mobile number for your account, check the box to opt in or out and select Save. If you don't have a mobile number, enter your number to add it to your account, then update your preference.
- For emails: Use the toggle to turn emails on or off. Your changes will save automatically.
- For push notifications: Use the toggle to turn on or off all push notifications or specific push notifications. Your changes will save automatically.

================================================================

## Delete your Chewy account

To permanently delete your account:
- Sign in to your account.
- Go to Settings & preferences.
- Select Privacy preferences.
- The option to delete your account is at the bottom of the page.
- Follow the prompts to confirm the deletion.

Once deleted, your account and all related information can't be recovered.

## Keep your account secure

Your account is the key to your pet's care, so it's important to keep it safe. From creating a strong password to using passwordless sign-in options, these steps help protect your information and ensure uninterrupted access to everything you and your pet need.

### Creating a strong password
A strong password helps prevent unauthorized access and protects you and your pet's personal information.

Tips for creating a strong password:
- Use at least 8 characters, including a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.
- Avoid common words or easily guessed phrases—don't use "password," "123456,"  or your pet's name.
- Don't reuse passwords from other accounts.
- Update your password regularly—you can do this in account settings.

### Adding a mobile number
This gives you a secure way to sign in with a one-time code if you ever forget your password.

To add a mobile number:
- Go to account settings.
- Select Edit next to the phone number field.
- Enter your mobile number.
- Enter the verification code sent to your phone to confirm it.

Once verified, you can use your phone to sign in or recover your account in the future.

### Adding a passkey
Passkeys are a simple and secure alternative to passwords. With a passkey, you can sign in to your account with your fingerprint, face scan, or device PIN.

To add a passkey:
- Go to Passkeys in account settings.
- Select Add a passkey.
- Follow the prompts to register your device.
