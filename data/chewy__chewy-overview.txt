## Chewy+ membership

Chewy+ is a paid membership that gives you exclusive savings and rewards every time you shop.

With a Chewy+ membership, you'll get:
- Free shipping on every order—no minimum purchase required.
- 5% back in rewards on every eligible purchase.
- Exclusive offers and discounts just for members.

These benefits combine with any other deals or savings on an item, including Autoship savings.

Try Chewy+ free for 30 days, then membership costs $79 annually.
