## Create a Chewy account

With a Chewy account, you can set up and save on repeat deliveries, order prescriptions, chat for free with our licensed vet team, and more.

To create a new account:
- Go to the Sign in page.
- Enter your email address and select Continue. You can also create an account with your Google or Apple details.
- Enter your full name and select Continue.
- Create an account with a one-time code to your mobile phone or create a password (at least 8 characters long).

That's it! Once your account is created, you'll be signed in automatically.

### Tips
- Use a personal email you check regularly so you don't miss order updates.
- Create a strong password that's easy for you to remember but hard for others to guess.

================================================================

## Sign in to your Chewy account

You'll need to sign in to place an order, manage your pet's prescriptions, or view your past purchases.

To sign in:
- Go to the Sign in page.
- Enter your account email address or continue with your Apple or Google account.
- If you entered an email address, choose to sign in with your password, a one-time code to your email or phone, or a passkey.
- Select Sign in to access your account.

If you've forgotten your password, select Forgot password? on the sign-in screen to reset it.

One-time codes expire after 1 hour. If the code doesn't arrive, check your spam folder or request a new one.
